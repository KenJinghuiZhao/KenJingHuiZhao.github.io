1.App introduction:

KenTranX is a translator that you can use your mobile phone camera to 'see' any language then translate it.

Key features:
  • Support words captured from camera, and image/text shared from other apps.
  • Most convinience to use.
  • Support Chinese, Japanese, Korean, English, France, Germany language
  • Support self-defined online dictionaries.
